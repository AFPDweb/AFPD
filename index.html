Welcome to Minecraft Survival City! (Name coming soon). This server is owned by PandaPaws24, and the Players are:
PandaPaws24, MemeACream, CrazPanda64
The server was originally made for CrazPanda64's youtube channel, CrazyGuy66. Soon after MemeACream and PandaPaws24 got bored, and decided to start building a city. This page will keep you up to date with what is happening. Below you will find dates and what we did, with the newest date always at the top. To the right you will see a gallery, with the oldest pictures at the bottom of the Gallery, and newest at the top, and make sure to scroll down to see all of the pictures!
This website will be updated in the future to include interactive parts, such as an idea panel for you to send in ideas that then might be built.

31/01/2021:
The first tall building needs a roof and an interior done. The Tools and Armour Shop interiors are not finished.
2 new levels have been added ontop of the Mob Farm. A villager breeder has been made next to the iron farm to make access of villagers easier. Infact, the breeder has already been used as The Libary has started the building process and 4 librarians (villagers with Lecterns) have been moved in.

28/01/2021:
The interiors of The Tools and Armour shop have started and soul sand has been gathered to create a nether highway using Soul Speed III books, that were from a Bastion (in order to get them, lots of death happened). There are 2 books so far.
They also located another Bastion that they may raid in the future.
Players MemeACream and PandaPaws24 also built a mob farm. The mob farm has 2 levels so far but others can easily be added ontop.

26/01/2021:
Planning has taken place and one of the tall buildings have been designed by PandaPaws24 and MemeACream. They do not yet know what is going inside.

19/01/2021:
Players have worked together to produce 4 new buildings.
2 have been completely completed and 2 need interiors done.
Players PandaPaws24 and MemeACream have mostly built the buildings.
More Players will be thought to join soon.
The City started planning around a 1.5 weeks ago and started being built 1 week ago.
There is now a total of 4 buildings done. The 2 completed buildings are the Fish and Chips Shop and the Bakery. The Clothing Shop and the Tools Shop need interiors done before they can open.
3 houses have been designed by PandaPaws24 for the players to stay in.
